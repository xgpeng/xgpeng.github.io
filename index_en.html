Work Experience

Dr Xingguang Peng is an associate professor in School of Marine Science and Technology, Northwestern Polytechnical University, China. He received the BSc degree in Electronics and Information Engineering, MSc and PhD degrees in System Engineering from NPU in 2003, 2006 and 2009, respectively. From 2010 to 2012, he worked as a postdoctoral researcher in Institute of Underwater Vehicle, NPU. He has been a faculty member of School of Marine Science and Technology, NPU since June 2012.
