Dr. Xingguang Peng received his B.Eng. M.Eng and PhD degrees from Northwestern Polytechnical University, Xi'an, China, in 2003, 2006 and 2009, respectively. From February 2015 to February 2016, he was an Academic Visitor in Department of Computer science, University of Surrey. In August 2018, he was a Visiting Associate Professor in School of Computer Science and Engineering, Nanyang Technological University. Now he is a Professor in School of Marine Science and Technology, Northwestern Polytechnical University, China. His areas of expertise include evolutionary computation based large-scale and dynamic optimization, self-organized multi-agent systems, swarm robotics and real-world problems.

Prof. Peng is senior member of Chinese Association of Automation (CAA), a member of the Task Force on Data-Driven Evolutionary Optimization of Expensive Problems Technical Committee of IEEE Computational Intelligence Society, a member of Intelligent Command and Control System Engineering Technical Committee of Chinese Institute of Command and Control, a member of Construction Robotics and Automation Technical Committee of CAA. He published a monograph on Dynamic Evolutionary Computation for Unmanned Systems, and more than 20 first-author papers in relative journals and conferences.
